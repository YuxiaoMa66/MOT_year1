The idea that "You cannot be criticised for your ethical views" isn't entirely true. It's important to challenge and talk about different ethical beliefs. This is how we learn and grow in understanding. It's true that "Everyone has their own ethics" because people's beliefs are shaped by their own experiences and cultures. But, just because everyone has their own view doesn't mean those views can't be questioned or discussed. Openly talking about ethics helps prevent our beliefs from getting stuck or unexamined, and it's crucial for living in a world with so many different viewpoints. So, while everyone has their own ethics, it's healthy and necessary to discuss and critique these views.
